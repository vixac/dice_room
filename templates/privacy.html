Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 April 2026

Who we are

Dice Room (www.diceroom.gg) is operated by Vixac Ltd. For any privacy-related questions, contact us at privacy@diceroom.gg.

What information we collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to run the service.

Roll data. When you roll dice in a room, we store:

  • The username you chose when joining the room
  • The dice type, result, and time of each roll
  • Any optional description you attach to a roll

This data is visible to everyone in the same room.

Cookies. We use two functional cookies:

  • username — remembers the name you entered so you don't have to retype it when returning to a room.
  • selectedDice — remembers your last chosen dice type for convenience.

These are strictly functional cookies. They are not used for tracking, advertising, or analytics.

What we do not collect

We do not collect your IP address, email address, or any information that directly identifies you. We do not use analytics services, advertising networks, or any third-party tracking tools.

Lawful basis

We process the limited data described above on the basis of legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) UK GDPR) — specifically, to provide the core functionality of the service you have chosen to use.

How long we keep your data

Roll data is retained for as long as the room exists. We may delete rooms that have been inactive for an extended period. Cookies expire when you close your browser and can be cleared at any time via your browser settings.

Who we share your data with

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold about you, and to object to its processing. To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@diceroom.gg and we will respond within 30 days.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision.
